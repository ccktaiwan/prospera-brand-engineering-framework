1. BRAND EQUITY COMPONENTS (NORMATIVE)
The Prospera Brand Value is calculated based on the following artifacts:
- **B-01 [INTEGRITY_INDEX]:** Aggregated SIT success rate from MND-L1.
- **B-02 [ASSET_BACKING]:** Total sum of SEALED IP Valuations in Repo #11.
- **B-03 [COMPLIANCE_PROOF]:** Verified ESG and ISMS certification status.

2. LOGICAL LINKAGE
The Brand System SHALL pull real-time telemetry from 'prospera-registry' 
to update the 'Public Trust Token' issued to stakeholders.
