Prospera Brand Engineering Framework
Phase 6 – External Partner Fatal Errors
────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION 1. DOCUMENT PURPOSE

This document enumerates fatal errors commonly committed by external partners
during Phase 6 (Scaling & Governance) that result in immediate scaling rejection,
rollback, or termination by enterprises.

This document does not prescribe remediation.
This document reflects enterprise termination reality.

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2. FATAL ERROR MATRIX (EXTERNAL PARTNER VIEW)
────────────────────────────────────────

+----------------------+-------------------------------------------+------------------------------+-------------------------------+
| Error Category       | External Partner Behavior                 | Enterprise Interpretation    | Scaling Outcome               |
+----------------------+-------------------------------------------+------------------------------+-------------------------------+
| Governance Override  | Bypassing agreed controls                 | Governance breach            | Immediate termination         |
| Scope Drift          | Expanding work beyond approved scope      | Control loss                 | Scaling rejection             |
| Hero Dependency      | Reliance on specific individuals          | Non-transferable capability  | Reject institutionalization  |
| Exception Creation   | Introducing special-case handling         | Governance erosion           | Rollback to Phase 5           |
| Trust Substitution   | Expecting trust to replace controls       | Unmanaged risk               | Scaling halted                |
| Cost Ambiguity       | Inability to forecast replicated cost     | Budget uncertainty           | Scaling blocked               |
| Process Deviation    | Ignoring standardized procedures          | Compliance failure           | Terminate scaling             |
| Governance Fatigue   | Increasing oversight demand over time     | Governance inflation         | Scaling terminated            |
+----------------------+-------------------------------------------+------------------------------+-------------------------------+

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3. ENTERPRISE RESPONSE RULES
────────────────────────────────────────

+-----------------------------+-------------------------------------------+
| Observed Condition          | Enterprise Response                        |
+-----------------------------+-------------------------------------------+
| Any single fatal error      | Immediate Phase 6 disqualification        |
| Repeated minor deviations   | Escalation to governance review           |
| Structural dependency found | Permanent exclusion from scaling          |
| Governance cost increase    | Terminate scaling activities              |
+-----------------------------+-------------------------------------------+

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 4. GOVERNANCE POSITIONING NOTE
────────────────────────────────────────

External partners are not terminated due to failure.
They are terminated due to ungovernable success.

Phase 6 tolerates no ambiguity in governance adherence.
