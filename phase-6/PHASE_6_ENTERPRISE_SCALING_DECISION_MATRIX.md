SECTION 1. DOCUMENT PURPOSE

This document captures how enterprises actually evaluate scaling decisions
during Phase 6 (Scaling & Governance).

This document does not redefine Phase 6.
This document does not describe execution.
This document reflects enterprise decision reality.

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2. GOVERNANCE STATE × ENTERPRISE ROLE DECISION MATRIX
────────────────────────────────────────

+------------------------+----------------------+------------------------+---------------------------+
| Governance State       | Legal                | Procurement             | Executive Management      |
+------------------------+----------------------+------------------------+---------------------------+
| EVALUATION             | Assess defensibility | Assess cost exposure   | Assess strategic fit      |
| REPLICATION            | Approve if bounded   | Approve if predictable | Approve controlled scale  |
| INSTITUTIONALIZATION   | Contractualize       | Standardize sourcing   | Endorse as core capability|
| GOVERNED_EXPANSION     | Revalidate controls  | Reforecast cost        | Approve limited expansion |
| SCALING_TERMINATED     | Freeze liability     | Halt commitments       | Terminate scaling         |
+------------------------+----------------------+------------------------+---------------------------+

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3. OBSERVED SCALING SIGNAL INTERPRETATION
────────────────────────────────────────

+-----------------------------------+----------------------+------------------------+---------------------------+
| Observed Signal                   | Legal Interpretation | Procurement View       | Management Decision       |
+-----------------------------------+----------------------+------------------------+---------------------------+
| Identical governance replicated   | Defensible           | Cost predictable       | Scalable                  |
| Additional oversight required     | Risk escalation      | Cost uncertainty       | Pause / Reject            |
| Dependency on specific individuals| Non-transferable     | Vendor lock-in risk    | Reject                    |
| Increasing exception frequency    | Governance failure   | Cost leakage           | Terminate                 |
+-----------------------------------+----------------------+------------------------+---------------------------+

────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 4. DECISION OUTCOME × ENTERPRISE ACTION
────────────────────────────────────────

+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------+
| Decision Outcome     | Enterprise Action                             |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------+
| APPROVE              | Proceed with replication under same controls |
| CONDITIONAL_APPROVE  | Limit scope, enforce governance checkpoints  |
| PAUSE                | Suspend scaling pending review               |
| REJECT               | Block scaling, maintain current state        |
| TERMINATE            | Revert to Phase 5 or exit collaboration      |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------+
